⚙️ My Engineering Process

Every project follows the same philosophy: understand the problem, design thoughtfully, validate experimentally, and continuously improve.

1

Identify

Understand the problem, define user needs, and establish engineering constraints.

2

Research

Study existing solutions, literature, and technical requirements before designing.

3

Design

Develop concepts through CAD, calculations, simulations, and engineering analysis.

4

Prototype

Build functional prototypes using manufacturing and rapid prototyping techniques.

5

Test

Collect data, validate performance, troubleshoot issues, and analyze results.

6

Iterate

Improve the design through multiple iterations until the engineering goals are met.
