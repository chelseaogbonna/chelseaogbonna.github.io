Leadership

I am currently the Academic Chair for the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) chapter at Northwestern University. With my position, I have led initiatives to strengthen collaboration among diverse engineering student groups, including SHPE, SWE, and WIC. I successfully organized a study session that united 50 students, fostering cross-club collaboration and increasing attendance by over 300%. By strategically promoting NSBE’s value and purpose through personal outreach and social media marketing, I increased meeting attendance by 60+ individuals, creating a more engaged and connected community. My leadership in these initiatives has not only improved academic support and networking opportunities but also fostered a sense of unity within the engineering student body at Northwestern, demonstrating my ability to lead, motivate, and drive meaningful change.
